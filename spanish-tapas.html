Spanish Tapas

About this selection

Tapas are the heart of Spanish dining—small, flavorful dishes made for sharing with friends and family. Our tapas selection brings you the authentic taste of Spain with both traditional and modern recipes.

Signature tapas

  • Patatas Bravas – crispy potatoes with spicy sauce
  • Gambas al Ajillo – prawns sautéed in garlic & olive oil
  • Chorizo a la Sidra – Spanish sausage simmered in cider
  • Tortilla Española – classic Spanish omelette
  • Pan con Tomate – rustic bread with tomato & olive oil
