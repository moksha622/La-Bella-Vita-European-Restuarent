German Bratwurst

About this dish

Bratwurst is a traditional German sausage made from seasoned pork and veal. At La Bella Vita, we serve it grilled to perfection with caramelized onions, house-made sauerkraut, and a touch of spicy mustard.

Traditional sides

  • Sauerkraut with caraway seeds
  • German potato salad
  • Pretzel bread with butter
  • Mustard trio (spicy, sweet, Dijon)
  • Seasonal pickles
